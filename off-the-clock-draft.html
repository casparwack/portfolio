About me

The real me

Outside of work I’m a 20-year-old uni student and the big brother to three other siblings. I currently live in a flat with seven boys, which means most weeknights involve training, uni work, and watching questionable romance movies. I’m a social person, so weekends are usually spent with mates and tend to be slightly unproductive. I also get pretty fixated on learning things, which means there’s normally a new hobby or interest on the go.

Overall, I’m a big believer in working hard while still making time for the things you enjoy.
