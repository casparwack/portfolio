What I do

I’m in my final year studying marketing and strategy at the University of Canterbury. I help businesses think through how they want to show up, and then help build the pieces that make it happen — strategy, branding, ads, social content, websites, and sales. I enjoy working across the whole process, from the early idea to the finished result.
